Green Salad

Bright spring greens and an incredible homemade maple balsamic dressing can literally save the day over and over and over.

Ingredients

  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Olive oil
  • Maple syrup
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper
  • Some greens

Steps

  1. Shake dressing Ingredients in a pot.
  2. Plop the greens in a bowl for tossing.
  3. Mix together.
  4. Eat.
